Why has my jasmine plant died?

13 Aug 2024, 00:00

Answer A lack of water can account for jasmine’s failure to begin growing again. Drought and dry soil prevent the jasmine from starting a new season’s growth, leaving the plant dormant and looking dead. Regular watering helps bring the plant out of its dormancy. Moreover, how do you revive a dying jasmine plant? Clip off all the damaged roots and repot the plant with fresh potting soil. If you don’t see any root rot, place the root ball back in the planter and cut down on the watering.
