Namaz Rakat Time Table 2023 – A Rak’ah is a single iteration of prescribed movements and supplications performed by Muslims as part of the prescribed obligatory prayer known as salah. Each of the five daily prayers observed by Muslims consists of a number of raka’at. Each daily prayer has a different number of rakats per prayer: Dhuhr: 4 rakats sunnah, 4 rakats fardh, 2 rakats sunnah, 2 rakats nafl. Asr: 4 rakat Sunnah, then 4 rakat Fardh.
