    China grass or agar agar is a favorite ingredient for pudding lovers across the world. Like gelatin, agar agar helps giving the pudding a jiggly and glossy effect. Mixing it with squishy cooked sago balls would make the softest and cutest desserts ever. Here we give you the recipe for an easy green sago china grass pudding for this festival treat.

    Ingredients
    1/2 cup sago
    10 gm china grass (agar agar)
    2 cups of milk
    1 cup condensed milk
    5 tbsp sugar
    1 drop green food coloring
    1 cup thick coconut milk
    1 cup melted jaggery

    Preparation
    Boil sago in two cups of water and drain it
    Heat two cups of water and add china grass to it to melt them
    Pour milk, condensed milk and sugar to a pan and heat it
    Add melted china grass to it and stir well
    Add green food coloring to it and sieve this mixture and set aside in a mold
    When this mixture is partially set, add cooked sago to it and refrigerate till set
    Garnish it with sago, coconut milk and melted jaggery
    Serve chilled
