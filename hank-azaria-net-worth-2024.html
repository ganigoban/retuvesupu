    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Hank Azaria was born on April 25, 1964, in Queens, New York. He developed an interest in acting at a young age and pursued his passion by studying drama at Tufts University. After graduation, he moved to Los Angeles to start his acting career.

    Azaria’s breakthrough came when he was cast in “The Simpsons” in 1989.
