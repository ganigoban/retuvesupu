    Carmella Mocks Video Game Nerds Who Gave Her Low WWE 2K23 Rating

    17 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Carmella stands out as a shining star in WWE’s women’s division, particularly in her recent performances on the main roster. However, despite her remarkable achievements, there may be some who remain unconvinced. That being said, it seems she mocked ‘video game nerds’ who gave her a low rating in WWE 2K23.

    WWE 2K23 has started revealing the value ratings for the wrestlers who will be featured in the upcoming video game set to hit the market on March 17. The ratings are based on their success rate and overall experience, with the highest numbers in the 90s being considered the best. Notably, Brock Lesnar has been assigned a remarkable rating of 97, while Becky Lynch is rated at an impressive 94.

    However, it seems that not all the wrestlers are content with the ratings assigned to them, as evidenced by Carmella’s recent comments regarding her own rating in the game, which was given to her by what she referred to as “some video game nerds.”

    Carmella received a modest rating of 79, which is respectable enough. That being said, she took to Twitter and made it clear that the rating does not affect her mindset whatsoever.

    After everything I went through a few months back, it feels meaningless and irrelevant to be upset over a trivial number some video game nerds assigned to me,” Carmella said. “My accomplishments, my resume and my talent should speak for themselves. I choose to be excited that I’m even included in a video game and I’m honored to be in 2k23. In 2023 I’m no longer fighting to convince people to see my worth. I know what I bring to the table and a sincere thank you to all of you who see it too. I love the WWE Universe so much!!”

    Zelina Vega was also irate over her own rating in WWE 2K23, which was considerably lower than Carmella’s. We will have to wait and see how the game will turn out once it releases next month.
