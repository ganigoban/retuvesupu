    Lisa is one of the hottest K-pop stars, period. The BLACKPINK member is known for her impressive dance skills, choreography, rapping and of course, her unique personal style. Not only that, the Thai singer is also the most followed K-pop star on Instagram — and has a pretty impeccable social media feed. She often shares photos of her daily life, vacations, the BLACKPINK members and most recently, bikini pics! Keep reading for all the times Lisa has sported a bathing suit. 

    Lisa has held the title for most followed K-pop star for years! With over 100 million followers, the only other K-pop idol to come close to her massive following is her own bandmate: Jennie Kim. And get this: the other two members of  BLACKPINK, Jisoo and Rosé, follow right behind.

    BLACKPINK first debuted in 2016 with their singles “BOOMBAYAH” and “Whistle,” and has since become the biggest girl group in the world. All of the members have also all enjoyed successful solo careers since their inception.

    Lisa has become a huge global superstar, nabbing multiple brand partnerships, launching a successful solo music career and more. On top of that, while the “LALISA” singer has become a role model internationally, but especially in her home country of Thailand. The young star spoke about the pressures of being a role model during an interview with Rolling Stone in May 2022.

    “Whenever I go to Thailand, there’s this new generation of babies that all look up to me: ‘I want to be like Lisa, unni!’ But when I look at myself, I am lacking in so many areas,” she revealed. “There are so many things I don’t know because I’m still young. They look up to me as an idol, but I still don’t think I’m perfect enough to be their idol. Am I there yet? Am I ready? I’m actually really curious. In what ways am I an idol to those kids? I still feel I have a long way to go.”
