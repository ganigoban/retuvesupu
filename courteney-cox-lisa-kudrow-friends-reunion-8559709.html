    There was more than one Friends mini-reunion this week — and fans can't get enough. This morning, Jennifer Aniston and David Schwimmer teamed up for a Super Bowl ad (thanks, Uber Eats for delivering our dinner and the laughs) and yesterday, Courteney Cox shared a reunion of her own with co-star Lisa Kudrow. In an Instagram dump that reflected on Southern California's current storm, Cox posted a photo that showed her and Kudrow posing together. While Cox had fuzzy black cat ears on, Kudrow looked happy and healthy (and dry!) as she smiled for the camera.

    "Photo dump with all the rain that’s dumping on LA," Cox wrote alongside the photos, which also included shots of her dog, a video clip of Cox getting into an ice bath, and a cameo from Jurassic Park's Laura Dern.

    Cox spoke about her love of cold plunges in an interview with Glamour, saying that while they're awful in the moment, they make her feel great long after she's escaped the nearly unbearable cold of the plunge.

    "I thought I would never do a cold plunge. I thought I would hate it, but I love it so much,” she said. “I feel great for hours afterwards. So, as much as you dread getting in, you know that you’re going to feel so good.”

    The IG post comes nearly a year after Aniston, Cox, and Kudrow came together for a ceremony to dedicate Cox's star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame back in Feb. 2023. Fans can see the two stars without much of a trek: Cox's star is at 6284 Hollywood Blvd., near Vine Street, which is just "a few feet away from" Aniston's, according to Ana Martinez, the producer of the Hollywood Walk of Fame. During the ceremony, the three Friends co-stars wore coordinating long black coats to keep the chill away (it was uncharacteristically cold for California that day).
