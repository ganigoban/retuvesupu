      Keira Knightley showed off more than her mega-watt smile as she stepped out with new fiancé James Righton in London this week. The 27-year-old actress also gave a flash of her glittering engagement ring.The ring is a single circular diamond set into a thin gold band.Keira could barely keep her eyes off her husband-to-be, following a romantic dinner date in the capital.

      The couple were spotted jumping into a taxi after dining together at The Wolseley in Mayfair.And the Hollywood star lovingly wrapped her leg around Klaxons musician James, 28, as they shared a joke together.Last week a spokesperson for Keira confirmed that she and James were engaged to be married after 15 months together.They were introduced by mutual friend Alexa Chung following the actress' five year relationship with her co-star Rupert Friend.Since they started dating, the couple have kept their romance under wraps.But following their engagement news, James' father spoke of their delight saying: "They are a truly lovely couple and as you can imagine, they’re over the moon with this news. "You can tell with some people if they're made for each other, and these two definitely are," he told the .

      While he did not confirm where James proposed to Keira, he did say the location was a very beautiful city somewhere in Europe. "I've not heard anything about wedding dates or where the wedding will take place," he added. "Keira is filming in New York soon and James is busy in the studio…so we'll have to wait and see."
