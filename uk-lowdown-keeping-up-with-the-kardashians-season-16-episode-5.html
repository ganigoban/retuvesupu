    KUWTK season 16 is in full swing.

    Each weekly episode is packed full of Kardashian goodness with the blended family and all their babies serving sass all day.

    Every member of the family has got their own thing going on and it’s literally becoming difficult to keep up!

    Here’s the UK Lowdown for Keeping Up with the Kardashians season 16 episode 5…

    • A timeline of Caroline Flack and Danny Cipriani’s relationship
    • We found Courtney Green’s new marriage-material boyfriend on Insta!
    • Olivia Bentley reveals battle with alopecia
    Kanye West Screen Shot: KUWTK S16 E05 Legally Brunette – Hayu

    Kourtney heads to Mexico

    With Halloween just around the corner and two GQ cover shoots thrown her way, Kourtney Kardashian had a difficult decision to make in KUWTK episode 5.

    Kourtney wanted to make sure that she celebrated Halloween with her kids as Mason, her eldest child, is growing up so fast.

    By the end of the episode, Kourtney managed to pull off her duties as a mum and her jobs modelling.

    She and Mason dressed up in matching Halloween outfits and she flew out to Mexico city to slay all day for the GQ shoots.

    Screen Shot: KUWTK S16 E05 Legally Brunette – Hayu

    Kim Kardashian: Lawyer in the making

    This episode Kim Kardashian let everyone in on the fact that she’s decided to train to be a lawyer.

    She’s following in the footsteps of her father, Robert Kardashian Sr. and is completing an apprenticeship in law.

    In episode 5 Kim sat down with her husband, Kanye West, to vent her stress.

    With her fourth child on the way, she said: “I dont know what to do, how am I going to have another kid? How am I going to study? How am I going to do this all?”

    Keeping Up with the Kardashians season 16 episode 5 Screen Shot – Hayu

    Khloe Kardashian gets artistic again

    Scott Disick set up a whole art studio for Khloe Kardashian and advised her to wear her apron as a dress.

    He said: “I think you’re going to be this new sexy artist!”

    Scott spent the episode doing his best to get Khloe’s new paintings appraised by an art consultant.

    However, the feedback was worse than anyone could have imagined. The art critic said that a three-year-old could have done a better job and that the paintings were probably worth less than the canvas that they were painted on.

    It seems that things have taken a turn for the worst since her Art Vandelay days!

    Screen Shot: Keeping Up with the Kardashians season 16 episode 5 – Hayu

    Kris and Scott take an art class

    It wouldn’t be an episode of KUWTK without ‘The Lord’ Scott Disick himself partaking in some kind of hilarious activity.

    This week he and Kris Jenner went to an art class.

    Little did Scott know that they were going to be sketching out a live naked man!

    This lot never fail to bring some banter!
