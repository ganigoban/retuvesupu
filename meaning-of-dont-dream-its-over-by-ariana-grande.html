    Meaning of Dont Dream Its Over by Ariana Grande

    12 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The Ariana Grande song "Don't Dream It's Over" is a beautiful and poignant rendition of the classic Crowded House track. The overall theme of the song revolves around the idea of unity, perseverance, and not letting external obstacles or negative forces divide people.One standout lyric that helps develop this theme is, "There is freedom within, there is freedom without, try to catch the deluge in a paper cup." This line conveys the notion that even though there may be limitations and challenges in life, there is still a sense of personal freedom and agency. It encourages listeners to think outside the box and not be confined by the restrictions of society or the world.Another important lyric is, "They come, they come to build a wall between us, and we know they won't win." This line reflects the current political climate and the divisive rhetoric that is prevalent in society. It suggests the importance of unity and solidarity in the face of these challenges. The idea of "building a wall" can be interpreted metaphorically, emphasizing the need to break down barriers and bridge divides rather than reinforcing them.The lyrics, "Now I'm towing my car, there's a hole in the roof, my possessions are causing me suspicion but there's no proof" convey the theme of materialism and the burden it can place on individuals. It suggests that material possessions can weigh us down and distract us from what truly matters in life. The hole in the roof could symbolize vulnerability, reminding us to focus on our emotional well-being and relationships rather than materialistic pursuits.Furthermore, the lyric, "Get to know the feeling of liberation and relief" emphasizes the theme of personal growth and self-discovery. It suggests that by confronting our fears and embracing change, we can experience liberation and find relief from the pressures and expectations placed upon us by society.Throughout the song, Ariana Grande and Miley Cyrus's harmonies and shared verses contribute to the idea that unity and collaboration are powerful forces that can overcome any obstacles. Their voices blend effortlessly, symbolizing the strength that can be found in coming together.In conclusion, "Don't Dream It's Over" delivers a powerful message of unity, perseverance, and breaking down barriers. It encourages listeners to look beyond external divisions and focus on the shared humanity that connects us all. Through unexpected and imaginative themes, the song presents an inspiring call to action, reminding us that together, we can overcome any challenge and build a better future.
