    Jenny McCarthy proved how gorgeous she really is without makeup in a stunning yet casual TikTok video shared in May 2021. The short clip showed the star revealing how she puts her long blonde locks into a bun with minimal effort, wowing as she ditched her usual glitzy look. Instead, she rocked an oversized dark gray hoodie with the words, "life is short don't be a d***" written across the chest while simply captioning the clip, "Bun club."

    But that wasn't the only time McCarthy has wowed on social media as her makeup-free beauty shone through. In February 2021, the star shared a video of her showing off her moves to Michael Jackson's infamous hit "Bad" as she goofed around on a balcony. Her skin glowed as she started off super close to the camera before walking away to show off her Jackson-inspired move in a baggy purple top and black leggings.

    But it's not just McCarthy who captures footage of herself au naturel. In January 2021, she shared a hilarious video of herself walking across the bedroom that was seemingly taken shortly after she woke up (complete with an eye mask pulled up to her forehead!) as she joked it's what her husband, Donnie Wahlberg, sees every morning. "Obviously NOT a morning person. Thanks for sharing your POV of me, Donald," she joked in the caption.
