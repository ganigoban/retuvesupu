    Spring is all about rebirth; nature wakes up from its long winter slumber and welcomes blooming flowers and sunny days. The same goes for fashion as we kick our heavy coats and big boots to the side and welcome sneakers, loose pants, and breezy tees back into our style rotations. But there’s one item that everyone needs: a slip dress.

    According to celebrity stylist and professional closet organizer, Caitlin Jaymes, a slip dress makes for an “effortless and comfortable” way to look chic in spring thanks to its versatile styling abilities. “Pair it with a blazer, pair of sneakers, ballet flats, or spring or summer sandals,” Jaymes said, explaining that there are “endless combos you can create.” Even better, slip dresses can be worn nearly everywhere, including brunch with the girls, a breezy park picnic, and Sunday mornings at home.

    5 On-Sale Spring Slip Dresses

    The ‘90s-inspired fit doesn’t have to cost you much, either — especially when the Amazon Big Spring Sale is going on now. For a limited time, you can snag satin, ribbed, midi, and long versions for as low as $15. Just shop them before the sale wraps, or you may be stuck paying full price. 

    Prettygarden Silky Midi Dress

    When you think of a slip dress, something similar to this Prettygarden option probably comes to mind. It’s the foolproof style that’s available in sizes small to 2XL and colors like purple, pink, and yellow. It includes a cowl neckline, spaghetti straps, asymmetrical hemline, and side slit for a leggy moment. The silky fabric skims the body, while the cinched waist delivers an hourglass figure that’ll wow this spring.

    Lillusory Bodycon Slip Dress

    The Lillusory slip dress gives me major Skims vibes, offering a fitted bodycon silhouette, thin straps, square neckline, and ankle-grazing length. The stretchy, soft fabric is ideal for max comfort, so you can wear it around the house, to dinner, or even a wedding like one pleased Amazon shopper. Shop the maxi dress in sizes small to extra large and seasonally perfect colors like sky blue, sage green, and white.

    Ekouaer Ribbed Dress

    For a shorter take on the comfy slip dress, consider this on-sale $15 Ekouaer find. Available in 18 colors and sizes small to 2XL, there’s something for everyone. I love the light gray hue because it pairs wonderfully with white tennis shoes and denim jackets; though, I’m just as excited about bolder colors like red and mint green. It’s so comfortable, some shoppers even use it as a night dress to keep their “boobs from falling out” — I won’t judge if you wear it out and instantly hit the hay in it.

    Finding the ideal slip dress is all about personal style, so choose an option that speaks to you! But I’m sure we can all agree on one thing: a good deal.
