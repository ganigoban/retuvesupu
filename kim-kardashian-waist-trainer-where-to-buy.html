    Get a Kim Kardashian-style waist trainer on Amazon for less

    Kim Kardashian made waist trainers corset-style belts to wear during your fitness routine to enhance your workouts, plus improve posture, support your back and help slim your waist a worldwide phenomenon.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Kim Kardashian made waist trainers – corset-style belts to wear during your fitness routine to enhance your workouts, plus improve posture, support your back and help slim your waist  – a worldwide phenomenon.

    And if you’ve ever wanted to try a Kardashian-Jenner style waist trainer – sisters Khloe Kardashian and Kylie Jenner are fans, too – for less, you’re in luck: I found one for a bargain price on Amazon. 

    (And if you're wondering what the exact brands the Kardashian-Jenners use, be sure to keep scrolling because I found that info out, too.)

    The ShaperX waist trainer, which comes in sizes from small to XXL, has an overall 4.2 rating with over 10,000 shoppers giving it a perfect five stars. 

    "SUPER CUTE, COMFY AND SUPPORTIVE," said one reviewer. "It's comfortable to wear and definitely a good start to begin waist training, really hugs those sides. LOVE 10/10."  Another buyer remarked in her review: "I use it while I walk/run and it’s perfect. As soon as I take it off, I notice my stomach is a little more slim, and after 2 babies, I need all the help I can get lol." 

    I actually bought a ShaperX waist trainer on Amazon based on the reviews after suffering from lockdown-related back pain in 2020. 

    Pros:

    • The quality of the ShaperX is very good, as is the support – if feels so much more expensive than it is. 
    • The double velcro closure means that you can fit it as snugly as you need to for the level of support you want. 

    Cons:

    • The main caveat is that the waist trainer seems to run on the small side, so be careful when choosing a size. While the belt is adjustable, be sure to take your measurements precisely with a tape measure before ordering.

    I've been using it for three years and it's definitely a pandemic era buy that I do not regret!

    Which waist trainer does Kim Kardashian use?

    Reality star and lingerie mogul Kim uses a waist trainer from her own Skims line – although the corset-style piece looks more like a piece of costuming from Bridgerton than workout gear! The Skims sculpting waist trainer is designed to cinch your waist, support your back and improve your core.

    Wondering where you can buy Kim Kardashian’s waist trainer? In the US or UK, you can get Skims waist trainers directly from the Skims website.

    Amazon also has similar lingerie corset-look style waist trainers – this one, with hook and eye closures, comes in sizes XS to 3X Large.

    Which waist trainer does Kylie Jenner use?

    Kim’s little sister Kylie Jenner is also known to be a huge fan of waist workout corsets, too.

    In addition to the Skims waist trainer, another belt the Kylie Cosmetics boss uses is the Sweet Sweat waist trimmer, which cinches your waist and promises to improve thermogenic activity during your workouts.

    You can get one on Amazon, where it's the #1 top selling waist trimmer, and it comes with a mesh carrying bag to wash and carry it.

    Which waist trainer does Khloe Kardashian use?

    Khloe Kardashian shared a workout video on Instagram in which she wears a fitness band from What Waist

    In 2021, fitness fanatic Khloe shared a sponsored post on Instagram about her waist trainer from What Waist, showing her exercising with BFF Malika while rocking the fitness band. "It definitely intensified the workout," Khloe enthused. In the video, she's sporting the black version but you can get them in an array of colours and in sizes from XS to 8XL.
