    24 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Kendall Jenner looked cool for the summer while in Calabasas this week.

    The supermodel was spotted stepping out of a studio Wednesday wearing a casual outfit made up of a canary-yellow cropped cami, high-waisted wide-leg blue jeans, a brown leather belt, and black square-toe slingback flats.

    She accessorized with tiny brown sunglasses and a thin gold chain, and carried a dark brown bag. As for beauty, she wore minimal rosy makeup and had her hair down in loose natural waves.

    Jenner's solo outing marks the first time she has been seen out since news broke that she split from her boyfriend of two years, Devin Booker.

    BACKGRID

    A source told E! News that though the model and the NBA star had a "really nice time" in Italy, where they attended Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker's wedding, once they returned to their daily lives, "They started to feel like they weren't aligned and realized they have very different lifestyles."

    The source said Jenner told Booker that she wanted some space and time apart to think things through, but they have kept in touch and have plans to hopefully reconcile in the near future. Per the source, they "both hope to make it work, but as of now, they are split."

    Though neither Jenner nor Booker have confirmed the reports of their breakup, Booker was noticeably absent from the Kardashians star's latest beach getaway, which she enjoyed with fellow model and friend Fai Khadra and singer Harry Hudson.
