    15 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Jordan Brand has a ton of highly coveted sneakers on tap for later this year, including a handful of Air Jordan 3s, several Air Jordan 11s, and a plethora of Air Jordan 1s.

    In fact, it is being reported that Jordan Brand will release at least 30 different Air Jordan 1s this year including some classic colorways and other unreleased models.

    Among the Air Jordan 1s releasing in 2018 will be this brand new "Game Royal" colorway that is now scheduled to release tomorrow, March 24th for the retail price of $160. The kicks feature a similar colorblocking to the beloved "Chicago" Air Jordan 1, except this pair sports a colorway consisting of Game Royal, Summit White, and Black.

    As Jordan Brand notes, “Following the blueprint set on the classic Chicago colorway of the Air Jordan 1, this makeup complements the Game Royal and white dominated upper with black details, including the tongue, sock liner, Swoosh and Wings logo.”

    The kicks will launch Saturday morning at 10am ET at all major retailers, including Finish Line, Foot Locker, Shoe Palace, Kick USA and of course, Nike.com.
