    Trippie Redd, whose real name is Michael Lamar White IV, is a 19-year-old American rapper, singer, songwriter, and musician hailing from Canton, Ohio. He is also known by various nicknames such as Big Daddy 14, Lil 14, Big 14, and Slippy Redd. Trippie gained immense popularity overnight with his rap songs like “Love Scars,” “Poles1469,” and “Dark Knight Dummo.”

    • Born on June 18, 1999, in Canton, Ohio, Trippie Redd is the younger son of his parents. His father was arrested for illegal activities, leaving his mother, “Momma Redd,” to raise him as a single parent. Following his father’s arrest, they moved to Columbus, Ohio, along with his elder brother “Dirty Redd” and their family. Trippie found inspiration for rap songs from his sibling.
    • Tragedy struck when his brother “Dirty Redd” died in a car accident, and in honor of his late brother’s dreams, Trippie decided to pursue a career in rap. However, not much is known about his other family members.
    • Trippie attended high school in Ohio, where he completed his graduation.
