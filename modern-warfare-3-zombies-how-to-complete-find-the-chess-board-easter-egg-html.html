    In Modern Warfare 3 Zombies, an exciting adventure awaits as you hunt for the elusive Chessboard Easter Egg. Your mission kicks off by locating the hidden chamber within the Urzikstan map’s mansion. Head to the secret coordinates H7 within the mansion to reveal the concealed room where the Chessboard awaits your arrival.

    Crucial Clues and Pieces

    Once inside, keep an eye out for essential pieces scattered across the map. These pieces hold vital clues for cracking the Easter Egg puzzle. Seek out the Pawn at I1, the Bishop at D1, the Rook at C5, and the Knight at FRemember their positions accurately for the next steps.

    Gathering the Components

    Now, armed with the locations, it’s time to collect the necessary components. Explore designated areas to find cellphones crucial for unlocking the secret room at HYour journey starts at D1, facing The Bishop boss. Defeat this adversary to retrieve the cellphone and use it to signal the mansion at H7, completing the first step.

    Move on to F6 to encounter The Knight boss. Utilize the cellphone obtained from The Bishop to track and defeat The Knight. This cellphone plays a crucial role in the following step. The Sniper, the third mini-boss at C5, awaits. Navigate the building, reach the top floor, and conquer The Sniper to collect the third cellphone needed.

    The last cellphone hides in an isolated house at I1, guarded by zombie hordes. Secure the final cellphone and return to the mansion area at HUnlock the secret room door with all components in hand to unravel the mysteries of the Easter Egg.

    Rich Rewards and the Ultimate Challenge

    As the door swings open, a treasure trove awaits inside the secret room. Discover chests filled with valuable resources, portable perk-a-colas, backpacks, Aetherium Crystals, and fresh Schematics. But stay sharp! One of the chests summons a formidable adversary, The King from Cold War’s Zombies mode. Defeat this boss to triumphantly conclude the Chessboard Easter Egg quest.

    An Alternate Approach

    For those seeking a different route, head beneath the Shahin Manor in the map’s bottom right corner. Here lies a concealed room that holds the key to unlocking the Easter Egg. This path offers a flexible sequence for collecting transmissions needed to unlock the Chessboard.

    Begin your journey with the Pawn transmission above the Orlov Military Base in a fishing shack. Brace for a confrontation with Disciples as you secure the first device. Move southwest to a hotel above “Zaravan City” to confront The Rook, guarding the second device. Defeat this adversary to obtain the necessary item.

    Head to the Orange Zone’s top left for the third transmission. Encounter The Bishop in an underground area, overcoming potential ambushes. Defeat The Bishop and secure the critical device. Lastly, track The Knight traveling in an Armored Car across the map, engage in battle, and collect the dropped device to complete your transmissions.

    Return to Shahin Manor with all acquired devices and unlock the hidden room. Inside, discover caches filled with self-revives, perk cans, weapons, and more. Be wary, as one of the caches conceals a mimic triggering The King boss. Prepare for an intense battle and emerge victorious in this thrilling alternative route.

    Final Words

    The Chessboard Easter Egg in Modern Warfare 3 Zombies promises an exhilarating adventure. Whether you follow the conventional path or opt for an alternative route, the rewards are worthwhile. Explore the hidden secrets and add an extra thrill to your gaming experience. Good luck, soldier!

    FAQs

    Q: What’s involved in the Chessboard Easter Egg quest in Modern Warfare 3 Zombies?

    A: The quest involves finding key pieces, collecting cellphones, and unlocking a secret room to gain valuable rewards.

    Q: How do I access the hidden room in the mansion?

    A: Collect cellphones from defeating mini-bosses in specific locations and use them to signal the mansion’s location.

    Q: Is there an alternative method to reveal the Chessboard Easter Egg?

    A: Yes, explore beneath the Shahin Manor, collect transmissions from different map locations, and unlock the hidden room for an alternate adventure.
