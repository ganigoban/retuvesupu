    With girls in the US starting their period earlier than ever, creating pads for younger girls, made from sustainable materials, is something Pinkie's founders are passionate about.

    What sets Pinkie apart from other female care brands is they are made from premium organic cotton and toxin-free, plant-based materials.

    Designed by two moms, these specially crafted pads are tailored to fit girls aged eight years-old and over.

    Unlike traditional pads, Pinkie pads come in custom sizes ensuring a comfortable and secure fit for all bodies.

    Each pad also comes in an individual baggie wrapper for easy and hygienic disposal.

    Pinkie pads are sold at over 500 Target stores, and you can pick up a pack of 18 for $9.99 online, which comes with free shipping for your products.
