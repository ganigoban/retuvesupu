    Tom Skerritt is one of Hollywood’s longest-serving actors today. He might have grown up but he is still very well known for his handsome looks and his rough and natural nature. Tom Skerritt, who has been in the film industry for nearly six decades, seems reluctant to quit his dream job anytime soon. Although he has yet to win an Oscar or Golden Globe in his acting career spanning more than half a century, he has already received the Primetime Emmy Award as well as several other awards.

    Tom Skerritt – biography

    He was born Thomas Roy Skerritt to Roy and Helen Skerritt on August 25, 1933 in Detroit, Michigan. His father was a businessman while his mother was a homemaker. His parents gave birth to three children with Tom being the last born.

    Tom Skerritt grew up in Detroit and attended high school in Michigan City. He attended Mackenzie High School in Detroit, graduating in 1951. He then attended Wayne State University, where he received his bachelor’s degree. Skerrit furthered his education by earning his master’s degree from the University of California. It was at university that Tom’s acting skills were first noticed, but before he began his acting career, he served four years in the United States Air Force.

    Movies and TV Shows

    Tom began his acting career in 1962 when he was cast as Sergeant Stan Showalter in the war film War Hunt . The same year he starred in his first TV show, Fight! , where he played various roles in all six episodes in which he starred between 1962 and 1967.

    Tom Skerritt has appeared in 63 films to date, and some of his best-known works come from films such as MASH, Big Bad Mama, Ice Castles, Top Gun, Alien, Kontakt, TED, Happy, and SpaceCamp.

    In MASH Skerritt was cast as Captain Augustus Bedford “Duke” Forrest. The film tells the story of three military doctors, adapted from Richard Hooker’s novel entitled MASH: A Novel About Three Military Doctors

    Tom starred in the 1979 science fiction horror film Alien Tom portrayed Dallas in the film. When first approached to be in the film, he initially turned the role down due to the film’s low budget and lack of a director at the time. However, after the budget was doubled and Ridley Scott was appointed director, Tom took on the role of Dallas.

    Wealth (income and earnings)

    Over the course of his 57 years in the actingbusiness, Tom has been able to amass a good amount of wealth for himself. He has a home on Lake Washington and one on Lopez Island where he spends his free time. At the time of the last check of his net worth, it was reported to be around $8 million.

    wife and children

    Tom Skerritt has been married three times. His first marriage was to Charlotte Shanks in 1957, with whom he had three children, Andy (b. 1962), Erin (b. 1964), and Matt (b. 1969). He and Shanks divorced in 1972. Five years later he married Sue Oran. He and Oran had one child named Colin (b. 1978). Their marriage ended in 1992. The renowned actor is currently married to Julie Tokashiki. The couple married in 1988 and they have an adopted daughter named Emi.
