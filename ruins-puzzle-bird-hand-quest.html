    Here's how to solve the ruins puzzle in the A' Bird In The Hand' side quest in Hogwarts Legacy.

    First Published May 5, 2023, 08:53

    The world of Hogwarts Legacy is brimming with challenging environmental puzzles, and you're bound to run into them sooner or later, whether you're completing main quests, side quests, or simply exploring the open world.

    As is the case in most games of this genre, some puzzles are easy to complete, some are a bit challenging, and others look intimidating at first but are rather easy to solve. The runes puzzle in the 'A Bird In The Hand' companion quest falls in the last category.

    If you're struggling to beat it, fret not. Here's our guide explaining how to solve the runes puzzle in the 'A Bird In The Hand' companion quest in Hogwarts Legacy.

    REMINDER - Hogwarts Legacy was due to launch on PS4 and Xbox One on April 4, but now won't release until 5 May 2023.

    Table of Contents

    How To Solve 'A Bird In The Hand' Ruins Puzzle

    As soon as you arrive in the marked location with Poppy Sweeting and Dorran the Centaur, you will find yourself in front of a cave entrance that's locked behind some runic puzzle.

    You will notice that there are seven rune symbols on the cave entrance, and only two of them are glowing. Remember those two glowing symbols. There are also two rotating pillars, a fixed pillar, and those same seven rune symbols on the ground next to the entrance. 

    The trick is to align the pillars in such a way that their light falls on the ground counterparts of those glowing symbols. Once both the two cave symbols and the same two ground symbols are glowing, the cave entrance should open.

    And that's it! Completing this quest will get you one step closer to completing Poppy's story arc and snagging the 'Beast Friends' achievement or trophy.

    Hogwarts Legacy Ruins Puzzle Solution For 'A Bird In The Hand' Quest FAQ

    How do I solve the runes puzzle in the 'A Bird In The Hand' companion quest in Hogwarts Legacy?

    To solve the runes puzzle in the 'A Bird In The Hand' companion quest, you need to align the pillars so that their light falls on the ground counterparts of the two glowing symbols on the cave entrance. Rotate the pillars until the same two symbols on the ground are glowing, and the cave entrance will open.

    What is the reward for completing the runes puzzle in the 'A Bird In The Hand' companion quest?

    Completing the runes puzzle in the 'A Bird In The Hand' companion quest progresses Poppy's story arc and allows you to earn the 'Beast Friends' achievement or trophy.
