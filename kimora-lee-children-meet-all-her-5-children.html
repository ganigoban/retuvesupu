    Kimora Lee Children: Meet All Her 5 Children – Kimora Lee Simmons, formerly known as Kimora Lee Perkins, is a prominent figure in the fashion industry and beyond.

    She is an American model, businesswoman, fashion designer, venture capitalist, entrepreneur, TV personality, philanthropist, and author.

    During her teenage years, Kimora Lee Simmons signed with the renowned fashion house CHANEL, launching her modeling career. She graced the runways for prestigious fashion houses like Fendi and Valentino and appeared on the covers of renowned fashion magazines such as Vogue and Elle.

    In 1999, Kimora achieved immense success by launching the global lifestyle brand Baby Phat, which garnered popularity worldwide. The brand became synonymous with style and glamour.

    Kimora expanded her endeavors into the realm of reality television in 2007, starring in the show “Kimora: Life in the Fab Lane” on the E! Network. The series chronicled her daily life as she juggled her roles as a businesswoman and mother. The show aired for four seasons.

    Beyond her accomplishments in fashion and modeling, Simmons received a Tony Award in 2003 for her work as a producer on the Broadway production “Def Poetry Jam.” This recognition showcased her diverse talents and contributions to the arts.

    Kimora is a philanthropist with a strong commitment to various causes. She has actively supported organizations like the Rush Philanthropic Arts Foundation, PETA (People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals), and amfAR (The Foundation for AIDS Research). In 2014, she established the Kimora Lee Simmons Scholarship Fund at FIT (Fashion Institute of Technology) to support students from underserved communities pursuing careers in fashion.

    Her humanitarian efforts extend to supporting refugees and migrant women and children in crisis situations. Kimora Lee has served as the Lead Global Ambassador for The Unmentionables, a nonprofit organization focused on providing essential hygiene products and support to migrant and refugee women.

    Kimora is also an advocate for gender equality in the workplace. She delivered a speech at The Global Innovation Coalition for Change (GICC), partnered with UN Women, to drive industry-wide change and advance women and girls in innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship.

    In collaboration with Smile Train, Simmons and Baby Phat launched the #AllSmilesAreBeautiful campaign in 2021. The campaign aimed to raise awareness and support for mental health programs for children with cleft palates.

    Simmons has made appearances in television shows, films, and music videos, showcasing her versatility and widespread influence. She has authored the book “Fabulosity: What It Is & How to Get It,” which received positive reviews and became a guidebook for female empowerment.
